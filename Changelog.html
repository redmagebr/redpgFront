0.28.2

Changes how screen real estate is divided amongst both sides.

Left Window will hog all the space available until resolutions 1356 pixels wide.

Beyond that, Left Window will grow at least four pixels for every pixel the right window grows.

Above 1626 pixels wide, Left Window will grow a pixel for every pixel the right window grows.

After Left Window stops being a space hogger, it will give back every pixel above what it needs to show a new avatar when showing the Chat. This means that the last Avatar shown will always align with the end of the Chat window.
