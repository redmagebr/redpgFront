Language Tracker added. Only Elvish and Binary are done, though.

/ling [Language], Message

Storytellers can also use languages as narrative through:

/lingsto [Language], Message

Special codes (Action, Thoughts, Important) will result in parts of the message not being translated (Working as intended)

/ling Elvish, My character speaks Elvish! Do you, [John]?

/lingsto Elvish, [The group arrives at the market place. You see a board which reads:] FOREIGNERS LEAVE!
